We want you to build amazing things using Strike48 and Prospector Studio. To make that possible, we've designed our legal structure to be **Developer First**. Here is the plain-English summary of our Terms of Use.

## You Own Your Work

**The Rule:** Anything you build, host, or deploy through our system belongs to you.

**Our Promise:** We make zero claim to your IP. We aren't here to claim your innovations or ownership of your software just because you used our SDK.

## Our "Orchestration Layer" Stays Ours

**The Rule:** While we give you the tools (SDKs/Apps) to build, the backend "engine" (our Orchestration Layer) is our proprietary property.

**The Benefit:** By keeping the core proprietary, we can ensure the system is there for you to use.

## Use Any License You Want

**The Rule:** Our apps and SDKs are designed to play nice with others. Whether you use GPL, AGPL, MIT, or Apache, you can integrate those libraries into your builds.

**The Shield:** Our legal structure ensures that your choice of open-source licenses won't "infect" our proprietary backend, and our proprietary backend won't "claim" your open-source frontend.

## Be a Good Human

**The Rule:** We don't monitor what you build, and we aren't responsible for it. However, if you use our system for illegal acts, hate speech, or harming others (see our [Prohibited Use Policy](/legal/terms-of-use/#exhibit-a--prohibited-use-policy)), we will cut off your access.

**The "Kill Switch":** We reserve the right to stop providing service at any time. We'll try to give you a heads-up if it's a business change, but if it's an abuse issue, the cutoff is immediate.
