Demos of StrikeKit's comprehensive red team features — engagement management, C2 operations, activity tracking, and report generation.

<div style="display: grid; grid-template-columns: repeat(auto-fill, minmax(280px, 1fr)); gap: 1.5rem; margin: 1.5rem 0;">

Creating and configuring a penetration testing engagement. Set objectives, define scope, and establish rules of engagement.
  **C2 Operations**

Starting listeners, deploying agents, and executing commands. See agent callbacks, command execution, and output handling.
  **Activity Timeline**

Tracking operations and auto-extracting targets, credentials, and findings. Watch real-time activity aggregation and intelligence gathering.
  **Pivot Chain Tracking**

Documenting lateral movement and attack paths. Map your progression through the target network.
  **Finding Documentation**

Recording vulnerabilities with severity ratings, MITRE ATT&CK mapping, and remediation guidance.
  **Report Generation**

Generating professional PDF reports with executive summary, technical findings, and evidence screenshots.
