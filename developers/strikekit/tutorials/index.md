## Available Tutorials

### [Creating Your First Engagement](creating-your-first-engagement)

Complete walkthrough of the engagement lifecycle from creation through execution to reporting.

**What you'll learn:**
- Creating engagements with templates
- Setting scope and rules of engagement
- Defining objectives
- Using AI-assisted planning
- Monitoring execution
- Generating reports

**Time:** 20-25 minutes | **Difficulty:** Beginner

## Tutorial Format

Each tutorial follows this structure:

- **Overview** - What you'll learn and prerequisites
- **Step-by-step instructions** - With screenshots and explanations
- **Best practices** - Tips for success
- **Next steps** - Where to go after completing the tutorial

- "Walk me through setting up scope"
- "What should my objectives be for an internal pentest?"
