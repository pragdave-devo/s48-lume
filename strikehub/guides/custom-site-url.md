If your organization hosts Strike48 Studio on a custom domain, you can configure StrikeHub to connect to it instead of the default URL.

## Step 1: Open Custom URL Sign In

On the StrikeHub sign-in screen, click **Custom URL sign in...** below the **Sign In** button.

![StrikeHub sign-in screen showing the Custom URL sign in link](/assets/img/strikehub/screenshots/custom-url-step-1.png)

## Step 2: Enter Your Studio URL

Enter your organization's Studio URL in the **Studio URL** field, then click **Sign In**.

![StrikeHub sign-in screen with Studio URL input field](/assets/img/strikehub/screenshots/custom-url-step-2.png)

The URL should be the full address of your Strike48 Studio instance, for example `https://CUSTOM.strike48.com`.
